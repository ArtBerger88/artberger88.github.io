About Me:

I am Art Berger,
I grew up in Inverness, went to IMS and Citrus High School. After school I started working as an apprentice Spray Technitian at Rainbow Springs Golf and Country Club.
I furthured my landscaping knowledge honing my craft, working at a couple commercial landscaping companies and learning the trade inside and out.
I then became a liscenced Spray Technitian with certifications in turfgrass, pest, and ornamenal weeds through the Department of Agriculture for Ohio, I've held this liscence for the last 8 years.

I consider myself a Lawn Engineer, just cutting grass doesn't quite describe my 'Works of Art'.
I take pride in my every job and the percision and artistic ability I present through it.
With this expertise and knowledge I return back home to Citrus County to start my own landscaping company -"The Art of Lawn Care"
I am not your average mowing and landscape guy, I am the exception.
