Meet Art Berger

Lawn engineer, Entrepreneur, and founder of The Art of Lawn Care LLC

About Me

I am Art Berger. I grew up in Inverness, attending IMS and Citrus High School. After graduating, I began my career as an apprentice spray technician at Rainbow Springs Golf and Country Club. Over the years, I furthered my landscaping knowledge, honing my craft while working with several commercial landscaping companies, sharpening my skill set and learning the trade inside and out, developing a true passion for the trade along the way.

I've held a license certification through the Ohio Department of Agriculture, with expertise in turfgrass, pests, and ornamental weeds. I've maintained this license for over eight years.

I consider myself a Lawn Engineer, just cutting grass doesn't quite describe my 'Works of Art'.
I take pride in every job and the precision and artistic ability I present through it.
With this expertise and knowledge I return back home to Citrus County to start my own landscaping company -"The Art of Lawn Care"
I am not your average mowing and landscape guy,
I am the exception.

Mission Statement

  • Provide the highest-quality work at an affordable cost.
  • Bring hard work ethic, punctuality, experience, expertise, trust and a neighbor-like mentality to the landscape trade in Citrus County.
  • Build long-term customer relationships through earned credit, artistic approach and a reputation built on high quality and value
